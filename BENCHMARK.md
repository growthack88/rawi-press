## 1. What Rawi Press Is (For Benchmarking)

Rawi Press is a **B2B news content syndication & distribution platform** — NOT a consumer news site. It sits between official sources (government, sports, events) and end-distribution channels (TV, journalists, creators).

### Core Characteristics
- **Gated B2B access** (invite-only, not public browsing)
- **4 subscriber tiers**: Government · TV Channels · Journalists · Content Creators
- **Multi-format content**: Video (HD/4K) · Text · Photos · Raw footage (Rushes) · Social-ready clips
- **AI-powered tools**: Voiceover · Tashkeel (diacritics) · Brand identity application · Translation
- **Broadcast-grade outputs**: 4K/HD for TV + 9:16 vertical for social
- **MENA/Saudi focus** with bilingual AR+EN
- **Entry strategy**: Sports (Roshn League, Formula E, Vision 2030 projects)

### What We Compete Against (Functional Categories)
1. **Global news wires** with B2B platforms (AP, Reuters, AFP)
2. **Editorial video/photo licensing platforms** (Getty, Storyful)
3. **Regional Arab news agencies** (SPA, WAM, QNA, SRMG)
4. **Sports video-as-a-service** platforms (WSC Sports, Magnifi)
5. **AI-powered content tools** embedded in newsrooms

## 2. Primary Benchmark Platforms (Tier 1 — Must Study)

### 🥇 AP Newsroom (newsroom.ap.org)

**Why study it:** The gold standard for news wire B2B platforms. Serves broadcasters, publishers, and digital media worldwide.

**Key features we should benchmark:**
| Feature | AP Implementation | Relevance to Rawi |
|---|---|---|
| **AI-powered search** | Descriptive language search (not just metadata) — finds specific things in images/videos even if not tagged | ⭐️⭐️⭐️ Must-have for Arabic content discovery |
| **Self-serve licensing** | E-commerce checkout for non-subscription customers | ⭐️⭐️⭐️ Aligns with tier-based access |
| **Organizational boards** | Save visual content for later collaborative use | ⭐️⭐️ Team workflow feature |
| **Story lines (AI)** | AI identifies different angles/themes in video coverage of major stories — customers see multiple narrative threads | ⭐️⭐️⭐️ Perfect for Saudi sports coverage |
| **Related content suggestions** | AI suggests similar videos in dashboard | ⭐️⭐️ Engagement booster |
| **Multi-modal unified platform** | Replaced separate AP Images + AP Archive with single platform | ⭐️⭐️⭐️ Rawi already doing this ✅ |
| **Human-in-the-loop standards** | No AI content delivered without journalist review | ⭐️⭐️⭐️ Trust & compliance — critical for Saudi market |
| **Archive depth** | 60M+ photos, millions of videos, text archive since 1985 | ⭐️ Long-term aspiration |

**Revenue scale benchmark:** AP generates **78% of revenue from content licensing**. 60M+ photos, 2M+ video stories in archive.

**Takeaway for Rawi:** The AP Newsroom design we already matched is validated. But we should add: (1) AI-powered Arabic semantic search, (2) "Storylines" view for multi-angle event coverage, (3) boards/collaboration features.

### 🥇 Reuters Connect (connect.reuters.com)

**Why study it:** The leading broadcaster-focused wire platform. Direct competitor to our "TV Channels" tier.

**Key features we should benchmark:**
| Feature | Reuters Implementation | Relevance |
|---|---|---|
| **Unified content destination** | 13M+ photos, videos, graphics, text in single platform | ⭐️⭐️⭐️ Core model |
| **Live video service** | Up to 6 concurrent live news events for broadcasters | ⭐️⭐️⭐️ Future Rawi feature |
| **Cloud-based delivery** | AWS TAMS (Time-Addressable Media Store) — content available near-instantly to partners | ⭐️⭐️⭐️ Delivery infrastructure gold standard |
| **Multi-workflow integration** | Works with both digital AND broadcast production workflows | ⭐️⭐️⭐️ Match for our Gov/TV/Journalist/Creator split |
| **Multi-format output** | Mobile streaming → broadcast HD simultaneously | ⭐️⭐️⭐️ Already in Rawi plan ✅ |
| **"Camera-to-cloud" speed** | Field footage → producers/customers in seconds | ⭐️⭐️ Aspirational — we target 5min |
| **Broadcaster-native distribution** | Content replicated to BBC, CNN, Sky, etc. through TAMS | ⭐️⭐️ Think: SSC, MBC, Al Arabiya integration |

**Takeaway for Rawi:** Reuters Connect's architecture is what we should aspire to build. The TAMS/cloud-native delivery layer is where broadcasters will eventually expect us to be.

### 🥇 AFP Forum (afp.com)

**Why study it:** Closest feature parity to what Rawi aims to be — multi-format, multi-language wire.

**Key features:**
| Feature | AFP Implementation | Relevance |
|---|---|---|
| **6 languages** | Text/photo/video/graphics in 6 languages | ⭐️⭐️⭐️ Bilingual AR+EN baseline for Rawi, expand later |
| **Daily volume** | 6,000+ new documents daily | ⭐️ Aspiration — Rawi currently 7 stories |
| **Archive depth** | 40M digital items | ⭐️ Long-term |
| **AFP TV (HD since 2007)** | 200 videos in 7 languages daily | ⭐️⭐️ Direct model for Rawi video ops |
| **Gated client access** | Newspapers/broadcasters get full access; public sees selected materials | ⭐️⭐️⭐️ EXACT match to Rawi's invite-only model |
| **Mobile-first delivery** | AFP Mobile white-label for mobile operators | ⭐️⭐️ Future opportunity |

**Takeaway for Rawi:** AFP's gated access model + public preview is exactly what we're doing. Their 7-language/multi-format operation is the scaling blueprint.

## 3. Editorial Content Licensing Platforms (Tier 2)

### Getty Images (gettyimages.com)

**Why study it:** Dominant premium editorial image/video licensing platform. Partnership model with BBC Motion Gallery, NBC News Archives, ITN, Bloomberg.

**Key features to benchmark:**
| Feature | Getty Implementation | Relevance |
|---|---|---|
| **Pricing tiers (5 levels)** | Single Asset · UltraPacks · Premium Access · Editorial Annual · Custom Content | ⭐️⭐️⭐️ Directly maps to Rawi's 4-tier model |
| **Rights-managed licensing** | Price varies by usage, geography, duration | ⭐️⭐️⭐️ Critical for Saudi content (regional rights) |
| **Lightbox feature** | Save/organize images for later review | ⭐️⭐️ Boards-style feature |
| **Media Manager DAM** | Enterprise DAM for organizing/controlling/measuring content | ⭐️⭐️⭐️ What government tier needs |
| **AI generator + modifier** | Nvidia Edify-based image generation with legal protection | ⭐️⭐️ Future add-on |
| **Partner collections** | BBC Motion Gallery, NBC, ITN, Bloomberg access | ⭐️⭐️⭐️ MODEL: Rawi could partner with SPA, MBC, SSC for exclusive content |

**Pricing reference (for Rawi tier pricing):**
- Editorial Annual: $5,750 for 600 downloads ($479/mo equivalent)
- Enterprise: $46,224/year average
- SMB: $11,009/year average

**Takeaway for Rawi:** The tier structure and rights-managed vs royalty-free model is mature. Partner collections (like Getty × BBC) could become Rawi × Al Hilal FC, Rawi × Qiddiya, Rawi × MiSK.

---

### Storyful (storyful.com)

**Why study it:** Specialist in social video verification & UGC licensing — a growing adjacent category Rawi could enter.

**Key features:**
| Feature | Storyful Implementation | Relevance |
|---|---|---|
| **Social verification** | Journalist-led verification of UGC before licensing | ⭐️⭐️⭐️ Critical trust differentiator |
| **Newswire service** | Real-time dashboard with verified content feed | ⭐️⭐️⭐️ Direct feature match |
| **Rights clearance** | Connects with UGC creators, clears rights, licenses to media | ⭐️⭐️ Long-term opportunity |
| **Multi-format brokerage** | Editorial + commercial licensing | ⭐️⭐️ Tier differentiation |
| **Verification tools** | Reverse image search, EXIF analysis, Google Earth geolocation | ⭐️⭐️⭐️ Technology stack reference |
| **AI integration** | Pilots since April 2023 for faster analysis with human oversight | ⭐️⭐️ Follows AP model |

**Clients:** CNN, ABC, Fox, NYT, WaPo, WSJ, BBC, Sky News, Reuters

**Takeaway for Rawi:** Storyful's model of "journalist-verified social content" is a blue ocean in Arab market. MENA has tons of UGC around big events (Riyadh Season, football matches, Hajj, Formula E). Rawi could differentiate by being the **trusted Arabic UGC wire** for regional broadcasters.

## 4. Regional Benchmark Platforms (Tier 3)

### Saudi Press Agency / SPA (spa.gov.sa)

**Why study it:** Government-owned agency — Rawi's most direct national competitor (but Rawi is privately positioned).

**What SPA does well:**
- Official government coverage (royal decrees, cabinet meetings)
- 5 languages (AR, EN, FR, RU, ZH, FA)
- Royal decrees distribution
- Saudi Photo Platform (launched June 2025, Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0)
- Scientific research portal
- SPA Academy for News Training

**Where SPA has gaps that Rawi can exploit:**
| Gap | Rawi Opportunity |
|---|---|
| ❌ No modern tiered subscription model | ✅ Rawi has 4 clear commercial tiers |
| ❌ No AI tools (voiceover, tashkeel, branding) | ✅ Rawi AI-first |
| ❌ No social-media-ready formats | ✅ Rawi has 9:16 vertical exports |
| ❌ Government-only editorial voice | ✅ Rawi covers sports, Vision 2030, private sector |
| ❌ No broadcaster-grade rushes/raw footage | ✅ Rawi offers Rushes tier for TV channels |
| ❌ Limited video/multimedia beyond text | ✅ Rawi is video-first |
| ❌ No self-serve dashboard for creators | ✅ Rawi content creator tier fills this |

**Takeaway:** Rawi is not competing with SPA — it's the **commercial, agile, multi-tier alternative** that SPA was never designed to be. Position as complementary: SPA for official government news, Rawi for sports/entertainment/Vision 2030/commercial content.

---

### SRMG (Saudi Research & Media Group)

**Why study it:** Largest integrated media group in MENA. Owns Asharq News, Asharq Business (with Bloomberg), Arab News, Al Eqtisadiah, Sayidaty, Hia.

**Strengths:**
- 30+ outlets across 4 continents, 7 languages
- Syndication rights with NYT, The Guardian, Bloomberg, Tribune Media
- SRMG Studios for content production
- Asharq Network broadcast operation

**What Rawi can learn:**
- Cross-platform distribution (same story across multiple outlets)
- Premium content partnerships (Bloomberg-style co-brand)
- Multi-language editorial desks

**Takeaway:** SRMG is a potential **customer/partner**, not competitor. Rawi could supply SRMG's Asharq News with broadcast-ready sports footage.

---

### Arab Newswire (arabnewswire.com)

**Why study it:** Press-release distribution service for MENA. Different model but overlapping customer base.

**What they offer:**
- Press release distribution in GCC/MENA
- Multimedia integration (images, videos, YouTube embeds)
- Expedited same-day distribution ($99/release)
- Industry/country targeting

**Takeaway:** Arab Newswire is PR-side, Rawi is editorial-side. Not a direct competitor, but indicates the MENA B2B content market has demand for paid distribution services.

## 5. Specialized Adjacent Platforms (Tier 4 — Feature Reference)

### WSC Sports (wsc-sports.com)

**Why study it:** AI-tailored sports content management, creation, and distribution. Used by leagues, teams, and broadcasters globally.

**Features Rawi should consider borrowing:**
- AI-driven content tailoring by platform (TikTok, Instagram, TV)
- "1000+ highlight packages in minutes" automation
- Personalization down to individual fan level
- Multi-format distribution from single event

**Takeaway:** WSC Sports is the gold standard for **sports-to-multi-platform-content** automation. Rawi's AI tools (voiceover, branding, 9:16) should aim for this quality level for the Roshn League/Formula E content.

---

### Magnifi (magnifi.ai)

**Why study it:** Automated sports highlights via AI. Used by Mountain West, Euro 2024 coverage.

**Features worth noting:**
- Accepts HLS, RTMP, SRT feeds for live broadcast
- MP4 ingestion for recorded content
- API-based integrations
- Real-time AI output dashboard

**Takeaway:** For Rawi's sports entry strategy, Magnifi shows the **technical ingestion pipeline** broadcasters expect. Rawi should offer similar API ingestion for partner sources (SPL clubs, Formula E organizers).

---

### Highlightly (highlightly.net)

**Why study it:** Combines match data + video highlights via REST API in JSON. Cost-effective alternative to Sportradar.

**Takeaway:** Confirms the **sports data + video API bundling** business model. Rawi could offer broadcasters a combined feed: match data + verified clips + rushes.

## 7. Rawi Press's Unique Position

### What NO competitor offers today:
1. **AR-first AI tools** (tashkeel, voiceover in Arabic dialects, Arabic-context translation)
2. **Saudi/Gulf editorial voice** in a commercial wire service (SPA is government-only)
3. **4-tier structure matching MENA media landscape** (Gov + TV + Journalists + Creators)
4. **Sports-first entry** with Vision 2030 project coverage built in
5. **Brand identity application** (each publisher gets content in their own visual style)

### Where Rawi is ahead of regional competitors:
- Modern commercial positioning (SPA is government)
- Video-first (most Arab agencies are text-first)
- Social-ready outputs built-in (9:16 vertical, captions, vertical highlights)
- Tier-based subscription model (most regional agencies are flat-fee government deals)

### Where Rawi needs to catch up (benchmark gaps):
1. **AI semantic search** for content discovery (AP leads here)
2. **Self-serve licensing e-commerce** (Getty, AP lead)
3. **Live video streaming** (Reuters leads)
4. **Archive depth** (5M+ target vs Rawi's current 7 stories)
5. **Partner collections** (Getty × BBC model — Rawi should pursue Rawi × SPL, Rawi × Qiddiya)
6. **API for broadcasters** (TAMS/cloud-native delivery — Reuters benchmark)
